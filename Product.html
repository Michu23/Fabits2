Exchange Traded
Funds (ETFs).

Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) are a cost-effective way to build long-term wealth. ETFs operate in a similar manner as mutual funds, but unlike MFs they can be purchased or sold on a stock exchange, and they have a lower expense ratio (cost to manage assets and broker commissions) compared to mutual funds. ETFs are also a low-risk investment as they are low-cost and largely index funds.
