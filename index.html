We are a goal based investing platform. We help manage your money better by helping you define your goals, create an action plan to reach your goals and handhold you till you get there. We are a fiduciary, which means we act in your best interest.

We'll ask a bit about you when you sign up. Then, we'll help you set financial goals and set you up with smart investment boxes (SIBs) for each goal. SIBs comprise of assets that are

broadly diversified across markets, are expected to perform well over the long-term, keep fees low, and don't try to time the market. For most people, we believe this is the best way to invest in public stock and bond markets. Each goal needs to be treated differently and risk taken varies. Our education portfolio is more conservative than a car purchase portfolio because education is critical in terms of time. In the event of great volatility such as what we have seen during COVID, we can delay the purchase of your car but can’t delay the education for your daughter.

We also track your progress with our AI-tech driven Advanced Investor Assist System (AIAS) and ensure the investments are changed based on changing market conditions. This ensures, you get better risk-adjusted returns in the long run.

The most important determinant of wealth for investors is their savings rate and their ability to stick with an investment strategy. That’s why we provide a technology experience that is designed to help you invest long-term and stick to your plan.

You can start investing with as little as Rs. 1000. We recommend you start with a minimum of Rs. 5000 to get the best returns.

For your long-term financial needs (like retirement, next year's vacation, or child’s education), our investment strategy utilizes low-cost ETFs (exchange-traded funds) and an adjustable risk profile based on your goal type and how long you plan to invest.

ETFs have performed better than actively managed mutual funds. Also, it costs significantly lower than mutual funds. ETFs avg fee is 0.1% whereas Mutual funds charge you an avg fee of 2%. You could save as high as 1.5L every year. If you reinvest that amount and consider the compound of magic, the savings could be as high as 25L in 10 years.

ETFs do not pay commissions unlike mutual funds. Fabits is a fiduciary, which means we act in your best interest. We do not believe in hidden commissions so you can expect unbiased guidance.

Most financial institutes charge fees based on percentage of the amount invested. We believe this is not fair because everyone is getting the same service. By paying more, you are not getting any additional benefits.

For this reason, our fees are flat irrespective of the amount invested. We charge a max of Rs 30 as brokerage and a max of Rs 299 for Smart Investment Boxes. Taxes and other regulatory charges are applicable.

Fabits is committed to ensuring everyone has access to better financial planning and investing with expert access. So for those we very small corpus or SIP amounts, we offer discounts on our fees.
