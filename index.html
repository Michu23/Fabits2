When you define your goals, we match that goal with a smart investment box based on the nature of the goal and the time horizon.
Growth Smart Investment boxes are ideal for investments over 10 years. The equity portion in these SIBs ranges from 70–90% equity. Expected returns are 10-15%.
Balanced Smart Investment boxes are ideal for investment for 3-10 years. The equity portion in these SIBs ranges from 50–80% equity. Expected returns are 9-13%.
Conservative Smart Investment boxes are ideal for investments over 1-5 years. The equity portion in these SIBs ranges from 20–50% equity. Expected returns are 7-10%.

Each goal is different in nature and requires specially designed investment vehicles. Returns have to be accessed keeping in mind the nature of the goals. Our experts and AIAS tech will do the hard work for you.
